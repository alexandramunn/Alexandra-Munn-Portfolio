About Me

Hey y'all! I was born and raised in corn country, also known as Iowa but I relocated to Nashville in 2019. I attended two and a half years at Iowa State as an enlgish major but decided to expand my horizons and move to the south. Since deciding to pursue other endeveaors I've been in the service industry as a server and bartemder. Late 2022 I decided it was time for a change which lead to enrolling in Vanderbilt's full stack web development bootcamp. I wanted to not only challenge myself but set myself up for future success. I am so excited to join an ever growing and changing industry where I'll be challenged.

I spend most of my free time with my two rescue Pitbulls or diving into my seemingly never ending pile of "to be read" books. I love to travel when I can and have visited 5 countries outside of the US so far. A fun tidbit about me is that I've been a vegetarian for 8 years and counting! Because of this I've learned to get creative in the kitchen and love to cook different cultural cuisines.
